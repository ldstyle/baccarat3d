Baccarat Rules

The goal is to bet on which hand will have a total closest to 9.

Card Values: Cards 2-9 are worth face value. 10s, Jacks, Queens, and Kings are worth 0. Aces are worth 1.

If the total exceeds 9, the first digit is dropped (e.g., 15 becomes 5).

Payouts:

  • Player: 1:1
  • Banker: 0.95:1 (5% commission)
  • Tie: 8:1
